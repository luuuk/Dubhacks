SAVE: Empowering Through Numbers

Our mission is to empower sexual assault victims/survivors by providing a confidential, unofficial reporting service that notifies them if another user has also experienced sexual harassment from the same perpetrator.

We will then provide each party the opportunity to ‘follow up.’ If they choose to do so, a confidential UW advocate will reach out to them in order to support them in learning about resources as well as options to move forward, if they would like to take further action.

By providing awareness about repeat offenders, we strive to remind victims/survivors that they are not alone, and to empower them to take action.

Regardless of how the parties choose to move forward, we hope to convey the many resources that they can tap into.

We will never release your information, under any circumstances.
